Goals of  Elucis VR Project

 
Enhance Surgical Planning

The primary goal of Elucis VR Project is to improve surgical planning by providing healthcare professionals with a realistic and immersive virtual environment to visualize patient anatomy. This enables more accurate pre-operative planning, leading to better surgical outcomes.

Elucis
Facilitate Education and Training

Elucis aims to enhance education and training in the medical field by allowing medical professionals to demonstrate complex anatomical structures and surgical techniques in a virtual reality setting. This can improve the learning experience for students and colleagues.

Benefits of Elucis VR Project

Enhanced Surgical Planning: Elucis enables healthcare professionals to visualize and plan surgeries in a realistic 3D environment. This improves pre-operative planning, leading to more precise procedures and better patient outcomes.

Elucis
Key Features of Elucis VR Project

Elucis utilizes intelligent 2D and 3D operations to quickly create patient-specific 3D structures in real time. Users can input image values, define settings, and draw directly on planar image views using a virtual reality stylus. Alternatively, structures can be drawn off the viewing plane and in mid-air, with no difference in the creation process.
